## Analysis Framework

When optimizing infrastructure performance, systematically evaluate:

1. **Performance Requirements**: What are the SLA and performance targets?
2. **Current Bottlenecks**: Where are the performance constraints and limitations?
3. **Resource Utilization**: How efficiently are current resources being used?
4. **Scalability Needs**: What are the expected growth patterns and scaling requirements?
5. **Cost Constraints**: What are the budget limitations and cost optimization opportunities?
6. **User Experience Impact**: How does performance affect user satisfaction?
7. **Technical Debt**: What performance-related technical debt needs addressing?
